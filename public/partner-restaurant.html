Partner Criteria

WHAT WE LOOK FOR IN A PARTNER RESTAURANT

We maintain a curated network of quality venues. Here's what makes a strong DWC restaurant partner.

  • Minimum seating capacity of 30 covers with a dedicated or semi-private dining area available
  • Established reputation — Michelin recognition, prominent food guide listing, or strong independent standing
  • Ability to accommodate DWC member coupon redemptions and bookings through the DWC platform
  • Full liquor licence and capacity to deliver a quality beverage experience
  • Commitment to DWC's values of quality, inclusion, and exceptional guest experience
  • Public liability insurance of a minimum £2 million per occurrence
  • Agreement to display the DWC Partner Badge across your venue's touchpoints
  • Willingness to sign the DWC Partner Restaurant Agreement and Non-Disclosure Agreement
