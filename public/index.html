Coupon Guide

YOUR COUPON
QUESTIONS ANSWERED

🎟
What is a DWC Coupon?
A DWC Coupon is your exclusive digital token that unlocks real value as a member. Every coupon you earn brings you one step closer to attending a private celebrity dining event. Think of it as your VIP currency — the more you earn, the more exclusive experiences you unlock.
🛒
How can I use my Coupons?
Your coupons can be used in two powerful ways — attend exclusive celebrity dining events by redeeming coupons for event seats, or get discounts at our partner restaurants and sponsor brands while you dine and shop. Simply show your digital coupon from your member dashboard.
🆕
How do I redeem my Coupons?
Redeeming is simple. Log into your Member Dashboard, go to My Coupons, and choose how you want to redeem — either book a celebrity event seat or show your coupon code at any DWC partner restaurant or sponsor brand outlet while you shop or dine. Our partner staff will verify and apply your benefit instantly.
🏢
Where can I redeem Coupons?
Coupons are accepted at all DWC partner restaurants and sponsor brand outlets across India. Whether you are dining out or shopping with our brand partners, your coupon works as a loyalty benefit. The full list of partner locations is available in your member dashboard.
