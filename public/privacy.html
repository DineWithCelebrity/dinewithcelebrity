Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026 · Effective: April 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Dine With Celebrity Pvt Ltd ("DWC", "we", "us", or "our"), a company registered in Hyderabad, Telangana, India, collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our website at dinewithcelebrity.com and related services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal information:

  • Account information: Full name, email address, mobile number, city, and state when you register.
  • Profile information: Movie industry preferences, interests, and dining preferences you voluntarily provide.
  • Payment information: Transaction details processed via Razorpay. We do not store your card or bank details — these are handled entirely by Razorpay's PCI-DSS compliant infrastructure.
  • Usage data: Pages visited, vouchers viewed and redeemed, events attended, and activity timestamps.
  • Device and technical data: IP address, browser type, and operating system for security and analytics purposes.
  • Communications: Messages or enquiries you send to us via email or contact forms.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your DWC membership account.
  • To process payments and manage your membership tier and expiry.
  • To personalise vouchers, event recommendations, and partner restaurants based on your city and preferences.
  • To send you membership updates, event notifications, and service communications via email.
  • To calculate and credit referral points and rewards.
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or security threats.
  • To comply with applicable Indian laws and regulations.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Supabase (database and authentication), Razorpay (payments), Resend (transactional email). Each processes data only as needed to provide their service.
  • Partner restaurants: We share only your DWC member ID and redemption details with partner restaurants when you redeem a voucher — never your email or phone number.
  • Legal requirements: If required by a court order, government authority, or applicable Indian law.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active, plus a reasonable period thereafter for legal and audit purposes. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by emailing legal@dinewithcelebrity.com. Payment transaction records are retained for 7 years as required by Indian tax law.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use session cookies for authentication (provided by Supabase Auth) and minimal analytics. We do not use third-party advertising cookies. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect your ability to sign in.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, HMAC-SHA256 payment verification, JWT-based authentication, and row-level security on our database. However, no system is 100% secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

Under applicable Indian data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and personal data.
  • Withdraw consent for optional data processing at any time.

To exercise these rights, email legal@dinewithcelebrity.com with "Data Request" in the subject line.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered members by email when material changes are made. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
