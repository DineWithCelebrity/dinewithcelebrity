Who We Are

Dine With Celebrity (DWC) is India's first membership platform that gives fans the exclusive opportunity to dine with their favourite celebrities — Bollywood, Tollywood, Kollywood, Mollywood, Sandalwood, cricket, sports, music, TV and business stars — in real life, at premium restaurant settings across India.

We are not a fan meet-and-greet. We are a curated, intimate dining experience. Our members sit at the same table as the stars they admire — sharing a meal, a conversation, and a memory that lasts a lifetime.
